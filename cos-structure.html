COS Organizational Structure
Dean, College of Science
Dr. Wilma R. Lomangaya
As the Dean of the College of Science, Dr. Wilma R. Lomangaya drives innovation in scientific research and education. She ensures that the College fosters a learning environment where students are encouraged to explore, experiment, and contribute to the scientific community.
Program Head, Bachelor of Human Services
Prof. Florencia V. Bautista
Prof. Florencia V. Bautista brings years of experience in human development and community services to the BHS program. Her leadership ensures that students gain practical knowledge and a deep understanding of social responsibility, preparing them for careers in service and care industries with compassion and competence.
Program Head, Bachelor of Science in Biology
Prof. Alana R. Buenviaje
Prof. Alana R. Buenviaje leads the BS Biology program with a strong foundation in research and life sciences. She is committed to nurturing future biologists through hands-on laboratory experiences, fieldwork, and a curriculum that fosters curiosity, environmental awareness, and scientific discipline.
Program Head, Bachelor of Science in Mathematics
Dr. Jose E. Binaluyo Jr.
Dr. Jose E. Binaluyo Jr. is an esteemed academic in the field of mathematics, known for his analytical insight and research contributions. Under his leadership, the BS Math program emphasizes both theoretical and applied mathematics, equipping students with the logical and critical thinking skills needed in education, industry, and research sectors.
Program Head, Bachelor of Science in Psychology
Prof. Mannellie Liwayway T. Herrera
Prof. Mannellie Liwayway T. Herrera guides the BS Psychology program with a strong emphasis on mental health awareness, research, and personal growth. She inspires students to explore the human mind and behavior, preparing them to become compassionate practitioners and researchers in the field of psychology.
Program Head, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
Prof. Michael B. Francisco
Prof. Michael B. Francisco leads the Computer Science program with a focus on innovation, coding excellence, and real-world application. His vision is to shape tech-savvy professionals ready to meet the challenges of the digital era, blending core computing principles with up-to-date technological trends.
