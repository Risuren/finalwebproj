Student Organizations
College of Science Student Body
The Mother organization of the College of Science, COSSB plays a pivotal role in representing student interests and coordinating activities that enhance our academic and social experiences.
College of Science Athletic Association
If you're passionate about sports and fitness, COSAA is the organization for you.
Biology Students Association
For students pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Biology. This organization supports biology majors through study groups, research opportunities, and events that deepen their understanding of biological sciences.
COS Mathematics Circle
An organization for students pursuing Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, which fosters a community of math enthusiasts.
Buklurang Gabay Patnubay
An organization for Bachelor of Science in Human Services students. It offers volunteer opportunities, internships, and support for career development in human services.
Technology Innovators through Programming and Algorithm Sciences
The organization for Bachelor of Science in Computer Science students, TIPA focuses on nurturing tech talents. It provides opportunities for coding, software development, and participation in tech competitions.
Students and Advocates Responsive to Gender and Development
They often focus on raising awareness about gender-related issues, advocating for policies that support gender sensitivity, and empowering students to challenge stereotypes and discrimination. Such groups can also organize workshops, campaigns, and activities that educate the school community about the importance of gender equity and development.
Student Researchers' Organization
For those interested in research, SRO provides a platform to engage in scientific inquiries and projects.
Samahan ng mga Mag-aaral sa Sikolohiya Morong Campus
An organization for students of Bachelor of Science in Psychology. It offers a supportive environment for psychology majors, organizing workshops, seminars, and discussions related to the field of psychology.
The Essence
The Essence is the official student publication of the College of Science, giving voice to the thoughts, ideas, and stories of future scientists, thinkers, and changemakers. Driven by creativity and a passion for truth, this organization captures the pulse of the COS community through news, features, and literary works that inform, inspire, and empower.
