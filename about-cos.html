About College of Science

Overview

The College of Science stands at the forefront of scientific education and research. We offer a dynamic array of four-year degree programs that blend foundational theories with real-world applications, empowering students to become critical thinkers, problem-solvers, and future leaders in science and technology.

What We Offer

Our curriculum spans both the natural and applied sciences, with programs designed to meet the evolving demands of industries, research institutions, and the academic community. Students receive hands-on experience in modern laboratories, engage in collaborative projects, and explore innovative solutions to local and global challenges.

Student Life and Opportunities

    Students of the College of Science benefit from:
  • State-of-the-art laboratories and equipment
  • Internship and research opportunities
  • Student organizations and science clubs
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Science fairs, exhibits, and competitions

Career Pathways

    Graduates of the College of Science are well-prepared for careers as:
  • Researchers and Laboratory Technicians
  • Science Educators and Academic Professionals
  • Environmental Scientists and Consultants
  • Software Developers and Data Analysts
  • Healthcare and Public Health Professionals
  • Graduate Students in top local and international institutions
