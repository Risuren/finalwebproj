College of Science

~ About College of Science ~ Organizational Structure ~ Faculty and staff ~ Student Organizations ~ Events and News
Programs Offered

Programs under the College of Science includes:

Bachelor in Human Services, which focuses on preparing students for roles in social work, counseling, and community support through studies in psychology, sociology, and human development.

Bachelor of Science in Biology major in Microbiology, a program that explores the microscopic world, covering subjects like microbial genetics, immunology, and microbial ecology.

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, which builds a solid foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving through coursework in calculus, algebra, statistics, and more.

Bachelor of Science in Psychology, dedicated to understanding human behavior and mental processes, touching on cognitive, social, developmental, and clinical areas of psychology.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, which centers on the principles of computing, including programming, software development, algorithms, and computer systems.
